# 🧊 Fridge Grand Prix

> *A car-themed visual betting game where octopuses race against lava lamps inside a fridge.*

Place your Schmeckles, pick your racer, and watch the chaos unfold. Go from 1,000 to a million — or lose it all trying.

---

## Getting Started

No build step. No dependencies. Just three files.

```
fridge-grand-prix/
├── index.html
├── style.css
└── game.js
```

Open `index.html` in any modern browser and you're racing.

---

## How to Play

1. **Pick a racer** from the panel on the right
2. **Set your wager** using the slider or quick-bet buttons
3. Hit **RACE!** and watch the fridge decide your fate
4. Collect your winnings — or don't

The goal is to turn your starting **1,000 Schmeckles** into **1,000,000 Schmeckles**. Go broke and it's game over.

| 🐙 Octo Speedster | ×2.0 | Fast and consistent. The safe pick. |
| 🫧 Lava Lamp FC | ×2.5 | Slow but steady. Reliable glow. |

## Betting

- **Slider** — drag to set any amount
- **10% / 25% / HALF / ALL IN** — quick bet buttons
- Minimum bet: **10 Schmeckles**
- Potential payout shown before each race

Win big enough and you hit the **Millionaire screen**. Lose everything and you hit the **Bankrupt screen**. Both are worth seeing.

## Tech Stack

- Vanilla HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript — no frameworks, no build tools
- Canvas API for the race track and car rendering
- Google Fonts: **Indie Flower** (display) + **PT Sans** (body)
- Designed with a hand-drawn, sketch-on-paper aesthetic
